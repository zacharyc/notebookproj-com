The Notebook Project

A community created art project.

What it is:

It is a collection of notebooks completed by communities. The current project is taking place at Simple Merchant. The rules are as follows:

  1. Pick a page
  2. Write, draw, or decorate the page
  3. Repeat (option)

you can see examples on our instagram: @notebookproj.
